About Me!

Hello! My name is Bryan Jerger. I was born on Febuary 25th, 1989. I was born and live in Chicago, IL. I love to travel and play games. I have worked in the hobby gaming industry for almost 10 years and am looking to take the next step in my life in the tech industry.

I was interested in technology for a very long time. I originally went to Depaul University for IT, but life sometimes doesn't lead you down the short path. Due to unforseen issues, I decided to take a break and work in retail at a hobby gaming store. After several years, I worked my way to manager and in-house IT specialist. I now control the store's website as well as all the IT needs.

It took several years, but now I want to work my way back into the technology field. I always like challenges and I feel this is the next step!
